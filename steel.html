📏 Standard Steel Sizes Available

We stock and supply a wide range of standard steel sections. Custom sizes available on request.

🏢 Universal Columns (UC)

"H-beams" - primarily for vertical support

  • 152 UC 23: 152.6mm x 152.2mm (23 kg/m)
  • 152 UC 37: 161.8mm x 154.4mm (37 kg/m)
  • 203 UC 46: 203.2mm x 203.6mm (46 kg/m)
  • 254 UC 73: 254.1mm x 254.6mm (73 kg/m)
  • 305 UC 97: 307.9mm x 305.3mm (97 kg/m)

Applications:

Internal load-bearing pillars, "Goal Post" structures, multi-story frames

⚠️ Width equal to depth - great resistance to crushing loads

🔺 Universal Beams (UB / RSJ)

"I-beams" - designed to resist bending

  • 127 x 76 x 13: Depth 127mm, Width 76mm (13 kg/m)
  • 178 x 102 x 19: Depth 177.8mm, Width 101.2mm (19 kg/m)
  • 203 x 133 x 25: Depth 203.2mm, Width 133.2mm (25 kg/m)
  • 254 x 146 x 31: Depth 251.4mm, Width 146.1mm (31 kg/m)
  • 305 x 165 x 40: Depth 303.4mm, Width 165mm (40 kg/m)

Applications:

Floor joists, roof supports, "knocking through" internal walls

⚠️ Standard for horizontal spans - 6m, 9m, 12m lengths

⬜ Hollow Sections (RHS/SHS/CHS)

Steel tubes - high strength-to-weight ratio

  • SHS (Square): Equal sides (50x50, 100x100mm)
  • RHS (Rectangular): Unequal sides (100x50, 150x100mm)
  • CHS (Circular): Round tubes (various diameters)
  • Wall Thickness: 2mm to 10mm
  • Lengths: 6m standard (cut to size)

Applications:

Fencing, mezzanine floors, decorative architecture

⚠️ Used for aesthetic or structural framing

📐 Merchant Bar

Smaller profiles for secondary work

  • Flat Bar: 40x3mm up to 130x20mm
  • Angle Iron: Equal (50x50) or Unequal (100x75)
  • PFC Channels: C-shaped beams (various)
  • Lengths: 6m standard (cut to size)
  • Finish: Mill, Galvanized

Applications:

Connecting plates, bracing, lintels, corner protection

⚠️ Used for reinforcement and fabrication

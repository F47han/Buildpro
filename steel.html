📏 Standard Steel Sizes Available

We stock and supply a wide range of standard steel sections. Custom sizes available on request - contact us for your specific requirements.

🏢 Universal Columns (UC)

Known as "H-beams" - primarily used for vertical support

  • 152 UC 23: 152.6mm x 152.2mm (23 kg/m)
  • 152 UC 37: 161.8mm x 154.4mm (37 kg/m)
  • 203 UC 46: 203.2mm x 203.6mm (46 kg/m)
  • 254 UC 73: 254.1mm x 254.6mm (73 kg/m)
  • 305 UC 97: 307.9mm x 305.3mm (97 kg/m)
  • Grade: S275, S355

Common Applications:

Internal load-bearing pillars, "Goal Post" structures, and multi-story frames

⚠️ Width roughly equal to depth - provides great resistance to crushing loads

🔺 Universal Beams (UB / RSJ)

Often called "I-beams" - designed to resist bending

  • 127 x 76 x 13: Depth 127mm, Width 76mm (13 kg/m)
  • 178 x 102 x 19: Depth 177.8mm, Width 101.2mm (19 kg/m)
  • 203 x 133 x 25: Depth 203.2mm, Width 133.2mm (25 kg/m)
  • 254 x 146 x 31: Depth 251.4mm, Width 146.1mm (31 kg/m)
  • 305 x 165 x 40: Depth 303.4mm, Width 165mm (40 kg/m)
  • Grade: S275, S355

Common Applications:

Floor joists, roof supports, and "knocking through" internal walls

⚠️ Standard for horizontal spans - lengths available: 6m, 9m, 12m (cut to size)

⬜ Hollow Sections (RHS / SHS / CHS)

Steel tubes with different profiles - high strength-to-weight ratio

  • SHS (Square): Equal sides (50x50, 100x100, 150x150mm)
  • RHS (Rectangular): Unequal sides (100x50, 150x100mm)
  • CHS (Circular): Round tubes (various diameters)
  • Wall Thickness: 2mm to 10mm
  • Lengths: 6m standard (cut to size)
  • Grade: S355

Common Applications:

Fencing, mezzanine floor supports, and decorative architecture

⚠️ Often used for aesthetic or structural framing

📐 Merchant Bar (Flats, Angles & Channels)

Smaller steel profiles for secondary structural work

  • Flat Bar (FLT): 40x3mm up to 130x20mm
  • Angle Iron (RSA): Equal (50x50) or Unequal (100x75)
  • PFC Channels: C-shaped beams (various sizes)
  • Lengths: 6m standard (cut to size)
  • Finish: Mill, Galvanized

Common Applications:

Connecting plates, bracing, lintels, corner protection, staircase frames

⚠️ Used for reinforcement and general fabrication

🎨 Finishing & Custom Services

Manchester Steels provides several finishing options which are critical for building regulations and longevity.

🔩

Self-Colour

Untreated steel in its natural mill finish. Requires immediate priming on-site to prevent rust.

For Indoor Use Only
🛡️

Primed (Zinc Phosphate)

Sprayed at 100–150 microns to provide active corrosion resistance. Ready for painting on-site.

Recommended for Internal Extensions
✨

Galvanised

Dipped in molten zinc for maximum corrosion protection. Essential for external use or damp environments.

Up to 60-Year Guarantee
